# CarePod

### A space to pause, reset, and return.

## Problem

Modern workplaces prioritize productivity but often overlook emotional recovery.

Employees experiencing stress, anxiety, frustration, grief, or emotional overload are frequently left with few private spaces to regain composure.

## Solution

CarePod proposes repurposing existing office rooms into dedicated emotional recovery spaces without requiring specialized hardware or infrastructure.

Individuals may use the space to:

- Sit quietly
- Reflect
- Cry
- Vent
- Scream
- Regain composure

The objective is not to dictate how emotions should be processed but to provide privacy when it is needed most.

## Applications

- Corporates
- Startups
- Hospitals
- Universities
- Public institutions
